creamy instant pot pasta

Discription

creamy Instant Pot pasta with a marinara sauce mixed with creamy goat cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 28 ounce can crushed fire roasted tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 table spoon Balsamic vinegar
  • Spices: 2 teaspoons garlic powder, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups baby or chopped spinach
  • 8 fresh basil leaves
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 4 ounce goat cheese log

Steps

  1. add water, tomatoes, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, glaric powder, oregano, salt, spinach, fresh basil leaves and penne to the instant pot. Stir until all combined.
  2. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes; keep in mind that it takes about 10 minutes for the pot to "preheat" or come up to the correct pressure before cooking start
  3. vent left over steam and open the lid, crumble goat cheese in and stir, sauce will thicken as it cools.
